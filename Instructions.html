Instructions for the quiz are as follows :

  1. The quiz is of 2 min 30 secs only.
  2. The timer will start as soon as click "OK" in the popup box.
  3. The page will give you a remainder when 30 secs are left.
  4. The window will close automatically once time is up.
  5. There are a total of 25 questions.
  6. Questions will be picked randomly from question bank and displayed on your screen
  7. In case you don't know the answer you compulsarly select one option to proceed to the next question.
  8. In case you choose the wrong answer the page will display an alert box displaying "Loser".
  9. In case you chose the right answer the page will immediately take you to the next question.
  10. You need to score atleast 15 points to win the game.
  11. Do not refresh or reload the the page as this will reset the timer and score count both.
  12. You will be awarded 1 mark for correct answer and 1 mark will be deducted for wrong answer.
